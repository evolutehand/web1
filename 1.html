Vinnie Colaiuta

Percussioner International ~ Vol. 1 - No. 4, 1987

"I think the only thing worse than the music business is stand-up comedy.
It is tough to maintain any kind of job. You could be hip today and tomorrow you are nothing.
It is so transitory and trendy you just never know. This puts extra pressure on you because it's like a do or die situation
I can't really complain though. I bought myself a house and financially, I'm looking pretty good."

Vinnie is making a comfortable living in music. He has been busy with a tour and album deal with Lee Ritenour,
and before that, there was Tom Scott, Joe Cocker, Joni Mitchell, Chaka Khan, and the "Who's Who",
to name a few. He played on Barry Finnerty's record, Maurice White's record,
the Commodore's and Sembello's record. All this in addition to juggling studio gigs.
